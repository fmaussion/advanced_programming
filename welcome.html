Welcome#

Lecture notes of the bachelor lecture Advanced Scientific Programming, taught as an optional advanced course for students following a Master in Atmospheric and Cryospheric Sciences curriculum at the University of Innsbruck.

This lecture is tailored for a 15 weeks-long class, but these notes might also be of interest to other (geo-)scientists wanting to learn more advanced scientific programming skills. Welcome to you all!
